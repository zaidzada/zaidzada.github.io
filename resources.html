This is a collection of resources I found helpful during my graduate work. Many of them come from an excellent collection ACS collection, Mastering the Art of Scientific Publication which includes many "Ten Simple Rules" articles. I organized the resources into sections depending on the type of advice you may be looking for. If any links no longer work, please contact me.
