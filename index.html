I'm a first-year graduate student at Princeton University working with Uri Hasson. My background education is in computer science (B.S., JMU) and machine learning (M.S., Georgia Tech).

I study language processing in the brain during naturalistic settings using machine learning. I'm interested in the connection between how the brain and machines process language, and how we can use both to improve our understanding of the other. Another line of research attempts to uncover how different world views emerge from language, and how communication may affect them.
